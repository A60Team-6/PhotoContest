Contest Categories

Nature

A general category for photos that feature natural elements, ecosystems, landscapes and living things, without a specific focus on a particular type of nature.

Wildlife

Photographs of animals in their natural environment, without human intervention. This category includes wild animals, birds, insects and other creatures.

Macro Photography

Detailed and close-up photos of small nature objects such as insects, water drops, leaves and other miniature elements shown in macro detail.

Landscapes

Photos of wide natural scenes, such as mountains, plains, forests, oceans, and more. The category focuses on the broad scope and scale of natural beauty.

Aerial Photography

Photographs of natural scenes taken from the air using drones or other aerial vehicles.

Water Elements

Footage that includes oceans, rivers, waterfalls, lakes, or any water features.

Night Skies

Pictures of the night sky, including stars, the moon, and other celestial bodies.

Seasons

Photographs that emphasize seasonal changes in nature, such as spring flowers, autumn leaves, snowy landscapes.

Extreme Weather

Photos focusing on the power of natural elements such as thunderstorms, tornadoes, hurricanes and other extreme weather events.

Sunrise and Sunset

Shots that capture the magic of sunrises and sunsets.

Underwater

Photos taken underwater showing marine life, corals, and waters capes.

Mountain Peaks

Photographs dedicated to mountain peaks and their majestic views.

Deserts

Photos of desert landscapes, sand dunes and desert flora and fauna.

Forests and Jungles

Photographs of dense forests and jungles that reveal the richness of these ecosystems.

Arctic and Polar Regions

Pictures dedicated to the cold and snowy places like the Arctic, Antarctica and polar regions.

Why Participate?

Join the competition to showcase your talent, gain recognition, and win prizes. Every photo counts toward raising awareness of nature preservation.
